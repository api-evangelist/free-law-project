Jessica Frank and Jenifer Whiston attended Free Law Project’s first ILTACON in August 2025, where Jessica presented on The Accidental Project Manager, highlighting how unexpected project management roles drive collaboration and innovation in legal technology. Alongside her, Jenifer connected with leaders from major law firms, highlighting the essential role of partnerships in sustaining Free Law Project’s mission.
